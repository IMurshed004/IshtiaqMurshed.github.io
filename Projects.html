Undergraduate Thesis

A preliminary assessment - Benefits of introducing cold chain in leafy vegetable supply chain in Bangladesh

Jun 2024 – June 2024
Ishtiaq Murshed, Sadiqul Zishan
  • Detailed survey on the supply chain of leafy vegetables in Bangladesh covering different markets and collecting field data.
  • Sample collection of vegetables and analysis of the bacterial growth on leafy vegetables at different points of the supply chain, identifying where the introduction of cold storage will preserve the quality of leafy vegetables.
  • Calculation of the total carbon footprint increase due to spoiled vegetables, including transportation, fertilizer, and other resource wastage
  • Analysis of the cold storage design and calculation of the operating costs behind it to conclude our objective.

A Design of 150 MTPD 98 % NaOH Plant

Jun 2024 – June 2024
Ishtiaq Murshed, Rayhan Bin Masud, Sifat Kawser, KAM Kazin
  • A design basis definition for the process plant by analysis of the demand and supply of our country, the geological data of the proposed plant location, transportation network and raw materials sourcing.
  • Drawings of PBD and PFD of the process, followed by performing a material and energy balance of the full plant.
  • Designed the 1st effect evaporator that was being used to concentrate the NaOH solution, including full calculations of the measurements of different evaporator parts and proper materials selection
